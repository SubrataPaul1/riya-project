Favourite Animal: Dog

My favorite animal is the dog because of its gentle and loyal nature. Dogs are known for their strong bond with humans and deep sense of trust. They are intelligent, trainable, and always eager to connect. Spending time with a dog brings a sense of peace and joy. Their playful energy brightens even the dullest days. Dogs are protective, often sensing danger before we do. They offer comfort and reduce stress without needing to speak a word. Each dog has a unique personality, yet all show pure affection. Caring for a dog teaches patience, empathy, love, and responsibility. To me, a dog is not just an animal, but a true and constant friend.

Favourite Flower: Arabian Jasmine

Arabian Jasmine is my favorite flower because it brings a sense of calm and comfort. Its soft white petals reflect purity and quiet beauty that feels almost timeless. The gentle fragrance of Arabian jasmine creates a peaceful atmosphere wherever it blooms. It often blossoms in the evening adding a subtle charm to the night air. Arabian jasmine is deeply rooted in many cultures, valued for its simplicity and meaning. The flower is often linked to love, purity, and spiritual connection. Its presence in gardens and homes brings a feeling of warmth and harmony. I admire how something so small can hold such deep emotion and grace. The scent of jasmine always reminds me of peaceful moments and cherished memories. To me, Arabian jasmine represents beauty in simplicity and the quiet strength of nature.

Favourite Festival: Durga Puja

Durga Puja is my favorite festival because it is full of excitement and devotion. The whole place is decorated with lights, flowers, and beautiful pandals. People wear new clothes and go out with family and friends to see the idols. The goddess Durga looks powerful and graceful in every pandal. The sound of dhak and the smell of incense make the atmosphere magical. Everyone enjoys traditional food and sweets during the festival. Many cultural programs, dances, and songs happen every evening. It feels good to see people happy and united during this time. Durga Puja brings joy, peace, and a strong sense of togetherness.

Favourite Season: Monsoon

Monsoon is my favourite season because it brings a refreshing change to the weather. The cool breeze and steady rainfall create a peaceful atmosphere. The earth smells fresh, and the trees look greener and more vibrant. I love watching the rain fall softly on rooftops and leaves. The sky stays covered in soft grey clouds, making everything feel calm. It’s the best time to enjoy hot snacks and a cup of tea. Puddles form everywhere, and children splash in them with joy. The air feels clean and light after every shower. Farmers depend on the monsoon to grow their crops. This season brings a sense of renewal and quiet happiness.

Favourite Book: Parineeta

Parineeta by Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay is a beautifully written story about love, sacrifice, and societal expectations. It tells the tale of Lalita, a gentle and mature girl, and Shekhar, her wealthy and conflicted childhood companion. Their deep bond is tested by misunderstandings, pride, and class differences. The novel shows how silent emotions can be stronger than spoken words. Lalita’s quiet strength and dignity leave a lasting impression. Shekhar’s struggle between love and ego adds emotional depth. The setting reflects the conservative Bengali society of that time. Chattopadhyay’s simple yet powerful storytelling brings each character to life. The story moves gently but leaves a strong impact. Parineeta is a timeless expression of love restrained by tradition.
